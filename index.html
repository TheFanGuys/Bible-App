📖 About the NASB: The New American Standard Bible (updated 1995 edition) is consistently rated by scholars as the most accurate word-for-word English translation. It draws from the oldest known manuscripts — the Dead Sea Scrolls, Codex Vaticanus, Codex Sinaiticus, and the Masoretic Text — making it the closest you can get to the original authors' words without reading ancient Greek and Hebrew.

🧠 ADHD Reading Tips

  • Read one paragraph at a time
  • Highlight as you go — it keeps you engaged
  • Read out loud to boost retention
  • Ask "What is God like here?" after each passage
  • Take breaks every 10-15 min without guilt
  • Audiobook + follow along = best combo

📖 SOAP Study Method

  • S — Scripture (write the verse)
  • O — Observation (what does it say?)
  • A — Application (what does it mean for me?)
  • P — Prayer (respond to God about it)
